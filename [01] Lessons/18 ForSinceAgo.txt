For, Since and ago

we used for and since to say how long.

    - we use for + a period of time
    - we use since + the start of the period


ago

it means before now.
we use ago with the past (started/did/had/was)


Compare ago and for: 

    When did Rachel arrive in Brazil? 
    She arrived in Brazil three days ago. 

    How long has she been in Brazil? 
    She has been in Brazil for three days.
